What is Barya?

Barya Business AI is a SaaS-style founder platform that combines business planning, startup guidance, practical templates, and AI-assisted decision support in one place. It is designed for local-first work, so your planning flow remains private, focused, and under your control from day one.

Who is it for?

Students

Learn practical startup thinking with clear structure and simple workflows.

First-time founders

Turn ideas into real plans without getting stuck in complexity.

Small business starters

Track finances, plan operations, and grow with confidence.

People with ideas, no structure

Get step-by-step guidance when you know what you want, but not where to begin.

Why this platform exists

Business planning is often confusing. Most people have strong ideas but low clarity. Existing tools are either too complex, too scattered, or too generic. Barya exists to fix that gap by giving founders one practical environment where learning, planning, and execution stay connected.
