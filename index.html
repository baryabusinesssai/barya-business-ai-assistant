Barya is a business planning and AI-powered assistant tool that helps founders turn ideas into clear plans, practical strategies, and trackable execution.

What is this?

This is a business building platform designed for beginners and early-stage founders who need structure and clarity.

  • Track finances and savings
  • Generate business ideas quickly
  • Create business plans with templates
  • Learn step-by-step with startup guides

Why this tool?

  • Simple and beginner-friendly workflows
  • No login required with local-first storage
  • Works instantly in your browser
  • Focused on real execution, not theory
