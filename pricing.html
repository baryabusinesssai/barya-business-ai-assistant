Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Can I start for free and upgrade later?

Yes. You can begin with the Free plan and upgrade to Pro any time without losing your workspace data.

Is there a yearly discount for Pro?

Yes. Pro is ₹499/month or ₹4,999/year, giving you a lower effective monthly cost on annual billing.

What does “local-first storage” mean?

Your workspace is designed to prioritize your control and privacy, with data handling built for founder-first security.

How is Enterprise pricing decided?

Enterprise plans are customized based on team size, support scope, integrations, and deployment requirements.

Do you offer refunds?

If something is not working as expected, contact support and we will help resolve billing concerns quickly and fairly.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your Pro plan anytime from billing settings, and your workspace remains accessible.
