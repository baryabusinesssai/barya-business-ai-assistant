Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Your data belongs to you.

Open Workspace Contact Support

1) Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains, in simple language, how Barya handles your information. We built Barya for founders who want speed and control without giving up privacy.

2) Information We Collect

Barya is designed so your working data stays with you. That includes your:

  • Notes, ideas, and planning documents
  • Tasks, strategy drafts, and workspace content
  • Financial entries, runway plans, and forecasts
  • Chats and interactions inside your local workspace

3) How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to power your product experience on your own device, such as organizing your workspace, generating outputs, and helping you execute faster.

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not share your data for advertising
  • We do not profile you for third-party marketing

4) Local-First Approach

Barya is 100% local-first — no data is sent to our servers.

Your notes, ideas, financial data, and chats stay on your device. This is a core product principle, not an optional setting.

5) Data Storage & Security

Everything stays on your device by default. We do not host your workspace content on Barya-managed servers.

  • Your sensitive business context remains under your control
  • Security depends on your device protections (passwords, OS security, backups)
  • We recommend using device encryption and regular local backups

6) Your Rights

You stay in control of your information at all times. You can:

  • Access your data whenever you want
  • Export your data in supported formats
  • Delete your data anytime from your own device

7) Third-Party Services

If third-party services are used in the future (for example payments, analytics, or optional integrations), we will clearly explain what data they process and why. We only choose providers that align with a privacy-first mindset.

8) Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the revised version on this page with a clear effective date. We aim to keep changes understandable and practical.

Effective date: April 16, 2026

9) Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Reach out any time — we're happy to help.
